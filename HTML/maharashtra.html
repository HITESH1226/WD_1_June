📂MAHARASHTRA 

Maharashtra is one of India's most important states, located in the western part of the country. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, natural beauty, and economic strength.

History

Maharashtra is closely associated with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the great Maratha ruler who established a strong and independent kingdom. Many historic forts across the state, such as Raigad Fort, reflect the bravery and military achievements of the Marathas.

Culture and Festivals

The state's culture is deeply rooted in traditions, music, dance, and festivals. Popular celebrations include:

Ganesh Chaturthi
Gudi Padwa

Traditional art forms such as Lavani dance and Dhol-Tasha performances are important parts of Maharashtra's cultural identity.
